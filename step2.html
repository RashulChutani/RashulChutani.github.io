STEP 2 :

Know the UPSC Syllabus :

  1. Firstly refer the Prelims & Mains syllabus. Understand the subjects and sub-topics. It will help to make your preparation more easy. Available on UPSC website.
  2. NCERT Publication books for GS subjects like History, Geography, Science, Economics, Environment & Biodiversity, Polity etc. Refer old NCERT books (before 2005 edition) for History.
  3. After going through NCERT thoroughly, try to make some notes out of Environment & Polity notes.
  4. Then refer some advance reference books like M. Laxmikant for Polity, Shankar IAS notes for Environment, Ramesh Singh / Shriram IAS notes for Economics, Vajiram Class notes for world history, etc.
  5. Refer The Hindu newspaper daily for editorial part and make some notes, if possible. Otherwise make cuttings of paper. You can read online also, use Evernote software for making notes.

  7. Read some monthly / weekly magazines like Yojana & EPW. It will help you for answer writing.
  8. If you have completed your reading once, then refer Insights website for answer writing practice daily. They provide very good questions on current affairs. You can also check other aspirants answers on their portal.
